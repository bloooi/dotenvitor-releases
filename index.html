Dotenvitor

Keep Your Secrets Out of the AI's Context.

Don't hand over your sensitive .env files to AI. Dotenvitor lets you safely, easily, and directly manage all your environment variables in one secure place.

Download for Mac Requires macOS 15.0 or later
01

Instant Access

Open your project just once, and you're immediately ready to manage all your .env files.

02

Seamless Tabbed Editing

Automatically discover .env files hidden deep in subfolders and switch between them effortlessly with tabs.

03

Raw Text Mode

Prefer the classic way? Switch to the traditional raw text editor mode whenever you want.

04

Built-in Dotenvx Support

Safely decrypt and view original values key-by-key. Edit them, and they are instantly re-encrypted upon saving.

05

Smart Cloning for Templates

Duplicate your .env files with a single click—keeping all the keys perfectly intact while safely stripping out the sensitive values.

06

Comment-Based Grouping

Keep things organized. Dotenvitor automatically groups your keys based on comments (easily toggled off in settings).

07

Contextual Descriptions

Inline comments written next to values are beautifully extracted and displayed as helpful descriptions at the bottom of the screen.

08

Quick Uncommenting

Instantly spot commented-out key/value pairs and restore them to active duty with just one click.

09

Instant Finder Access

Jump straight to your project, specific folder, or .env file in Finder directly from the editor.

10

Duplicate Key Detection

Never overwrite variables by mistake. Get instant real-time alerts if you create a duplicate key.
